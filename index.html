The Product

Neurosight Platform Interface

Eye-tracking + AI MRI Analysis

Dual-Pathway AI Screening Platform

Neurosight integrates cutting-edge eye-tracking technology with deep learning-powered MRI analysis to create a complete solution from initial risk screening to clinical diagnostic support.

  • Step 1: Eye-Movement Screening - Users complete a 3-5 minute eye-tracking test through our online platform for preliminary cognitive assessment
  • Step 2: AI MRI Analysis - High-risk users can upload MRI images for automated analysis by our AI algorithm, achieving 97% accuracy
  • Step 3: Clinical Decision Support - Provides detailed reports and follow-up action guidelines to help doctors and patients develop intervention plans
  • Step 4: Continuous Monitoring - Subscribing users can perform regular follow-up tests to monitor cognitive trend changes

Our platform is designed specifically for elderly users with an intuitive, friendly interface. The testing process is simple and convenient, allowing preliminary screening to be completed at home without professional medical guidance.

Why Choose Neurosight?

For individual users, Neurosight provides a dignified, convenient first line of defense. No specialist appointment needed, no expensive MRI costs—preliminary risk assessment can be completed at home.

For healthcare systems, Neurosight optimizes resource allocation, reserving expensive MRI resources for those who truly need them, reducing wait times, and improving diagnostic efficiency.

For public health departments, our platform supports large-scale community screening, helping the public detect potential illness earlier and promoting public health initiatives.

"The 'golden window' for early Alzheimer's intervention often closes by the time of diagnosis. Neurosight's innovative dual-pathway screening advances diagnosis by years, creating possibilities for effective intervention."

Business Model

Revenue Streams

1. Hospital & Diagnostic Center SaaS

We provide the integrated screening and MRI analysis system to hospitals and diagnostic centers, charging annual SaaS subscription fees or per-analysis service fees.

2. Data Analytics Services

We offer structured, anonymized data analytics services on eye-movement and MRI data to pharmaceutical companies and research institutions, earning project-based technical service fees.

3. Direct-to-Consumer Services

Individual users can purchase detailed single-assessment reports or subscribe to monthly/annual cognitive health monitoring plans, creating a direct-to-consumer recurring revenue stream.
